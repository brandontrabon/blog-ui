ABOUT M. STIEG
What can I say about myself? Probably plenty, however, I should start from the beginning.
I was hesitant to start a blog. I am not a quick thinker when it comes to conjuring up stories on the whim. Actually, it was my sibling who could do that. However, I decided to give it a go, hoping along the way my writing could improve. I love to write and as a novice I do become restive at times. Sometimes, at night, I light a candle and from there I begin to type.
I do hope everyone enjoys them. Thank you.
